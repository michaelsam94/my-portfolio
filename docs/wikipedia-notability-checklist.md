# Wikipedia Notability Checklist

This checklist is for a possible future `Draft:Michael Samuel Naeem` article.
Do not submit the draft until the source requirements are met.

## Current Status

Status: not ready for mainspace submission.

Reason: the portfolio has strong first-party proof, but Wikipedia normally needs
significant coverage in reliable, independent, secondary sources. LinkedIn,
GitHub, Google Play, a personal website, marketplace listings, and a CV can
verify facts, but they usually do not establish notability by themselves.

## What Wikipedia Needs

1. At least 2-3 independent reliable secondary sources with significant coverage.
2. Sources should be about Michael Samuel Naeem, not just pages written by him.
3. Sources should have editorial oversight, such as reputable news, books,
   journals, conference profiles, major awards, or independent interviews.
4. The draft must be neutral, concise, and non-promotional.
5. Because this is about a living person, every claim must be carefully sourced.
6. If Michael or anyone working for him submits the draft, use Articles for
   Creation and disclose the conflict of interest.

## Source Quality Tiers

Strong sources:
- Independent profile in a reputable publication.
- Independent interview with editorial oversight.
- Major award or industry recognition covered by independent media.
- Conference speaker profile from a notable event, if supported by independent
  coverage.
- Book, journal, or news article discussing his work in depth.

Weak sources:
- Personal website.
- LinkedIn.
- GitHub.
- Google Play listing.
- VS Code Marketplace listing.
- Press release.
- Self-published blog post.
- Employer bio.

## Next Actions

1. Collect independent articles, interviews, awards, and conference profiles.
2. Add the best sources to `docs/wikipedia-draft.mediawiki`.
3. Remove any claim that cannot be verified by a reliable source.
4. Submit only through Wikipedia Articles for Creation, not directly to mainspace.
5. Disclose COI on the draft talk page if you are Michael or working on his behalf.
