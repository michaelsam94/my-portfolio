NotchCommand is an ad-free Android utility that turns your punch-hole camera cutout into an interactive shortcut launcher, battery status ring, and audio visualizer — without ads or telemetry clutter.

**Ad-free:** Completely ad-free — no ads, no trackers, no sponsored clutter.

## Overview
NotchCommand turns your device's punch-hole front camera cutout into an interactive shortcut launcher, status visualizer, and neon-glowing utility ring. 

Transform a static hardware cutout into a dynamic tool that keeps you productive. NotchCommand uses custom layout calibrations to precisely align a glowing ring around your device's notch or camera hole. 

🌟 KEY FEATURES:
- **Interactive Cutout Gestures**: Configure Single Tap, Double Tap, or Long Press gestures precisely on the camera ring.
- **Instant System Actions**: Trigger actions like Toggle Flashlight, Take Screenshot, Open System Settings, Go Home, Go Back, and Launch Camera immediately.
- **Battery Status Sweep**: Visually monitor your battery level with a sweep arc that wraps around the camera. It changes colors dynamically from bright green (charged) to amber (medium) and bright red (low).
- **Dynamic Audio Visualizer**: Watch the ring pulse and glow in sync with your device's media audio levels, creating a premium visual effect.
- **Charging Indicator**: A small animated dot pulses at the top of the ring whenever the device is plugged in.
- **Rainbow RGB Mode**: Cycle the status ring through the full HSV color spectrum with a gorgeous neon rainbow animation.

🔋 SMART & EFFICIENT:
NotchCommand is built with performance and battery conservation in mind. It uses passive hardware sensor listeners and a micro-sampled audio gate that halts automatically when the screen is off or the device is silent, ensuring zero background battery drain.

🔒 PRIVACY-FIRST:
NotchCommand operates locally. It runs without tracking, ads, or telemetry, only requiring permissions (like System Overlay and Accessibility) to display the ring and execute system gestures.

Upgrade your Android display visualizer. Calibrate, customize, and take command with NotchCommand today!
