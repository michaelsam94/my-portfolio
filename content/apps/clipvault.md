ClipVault is an ad-free, privacy-first Android clipboard history manager that encrypts captures on-device with AES-GCM and never requires internet permission for your clipboard data.

**Ad-free:** Completely ad-free — no ads, no trackers, no sponsored clutter.

## Overview
ClipVault is a privacy-first, ultra-secure local clipboard history manager and text automation engine designed to keep your clipboard data entirely offline and protected.

Unlike other clipboard tools, ClipVault does not require internet permission and never transmits your data to external servers. All clipboard captures are encrypted on-device using AES-GCM-256 cryptography backed by the Android Keystore (Trusted Execution Environment / Secure Enclave).

### Core Features

* **Hardware-Backed Cryptography**: Your clipboard history is secured using keys generated in your device's secure hardware enclave. Even with physical device access, your database remains encrypted.
* **Smart Regex Triggers**: Define custom Regular Expression matching rules to detect patterns like IBANs, phone numbers, tracking IDs, URLs, and emails. Run instant automation actions when a match is copied.
* **Transform Pipeline Hub**: Offline text processor to quickly format, clean, or transform text. Uppercase, lowercase, spaces trimming, minification, and custom regex-based replacements at your fingertips.
* **Favorites & Smart Tags**: Bookmark important clippings and organize your snippets with tags for rapid access.
* **Secure Wipe**: Instantly purge database records or reset configuration filters with one click.
* **100% Offline**: Zero tracking, zero network requests, zero remote storage. Your clipboard belongs to you.
